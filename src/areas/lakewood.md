Lakewood sits in a notary dead zone of sorts: big enough to generate constant demand — 150,000+ residents, a federal workforce, and some of the metro's most established neighborhoods — but most "Denver" notary services treat everything west of Sheridan as a reluctant add-on. We treat it as core territory.

## What Lakewood actually calls us for

**Workday signings near the Federal Center.** The Denver Federal Center puts thousands of federal employees on Union Boulevard, and federal careers generate paperwork — retirement and benefits documents, POAs before overseas postings, TSP and beneficiary forms that require notarized signatures. We meet in lobbies and coffee shops around the Federal Center and Belmar, and a lunch break is usually enough.

**Estate and senior documents.** Lakewood's established neighborhoods — Green Mountain, Applewood-adjacent streets, the mid-century blocks off Wadsworth — mean a lot of long-time homeowners doing exactly the planning everyone should: wills packages, trusts, transfer-on-death deeds, and medical directives. Many of these need witnesses in addition to the notary; we'll flag that when you book, because discovering it mid-appointment is how documents fail.

**Jefferson County court paperwork.** Affidavits and sworn statements headed to the courthouse in Golden. We're the notarization step, not the legal-advice step — and telling you that difference clearly is part of the service.

**Home equity and refinance signings.** Lakewood homeowners are sitting on decades of appreciation, and [HELOC signings](/services/loan-signing/) — often borrower-arranged rather than title-ordered — are steady work here. Flat package price, quoted before booking.

## Coverage and timing

All of Lakewood: Edgewater border to Bear Creek, Belmar to the Green Mountain trailheads, plus quick hops to Wheat Ridge, Golden, and Morrison. Same-day is the norm for calls before mid-afternoon. Colorado's $15-per-act cap on the notarization itself applies as it does everywhere — the travel fee is the variable, and you'll have the full number before anyone drives.
